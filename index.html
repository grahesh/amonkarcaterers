About Us

"Good Food for Good Celebration" - That's our motto, and we stand by it.

Weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, parties or conferences we cater to all your culinary needs with finger licking food at reasonable rates making the occasion a lasting and memorable experience. Choose from our wide range of continental, Indian and Chinese cuisine to make a buffet of your choice, or just put together a package a la carte whatever may be the occasion, from a small casual gathering to a lavish party and from a wedding to a corporate event. Rely on our experience to make your dream event one of the most cherished memories of your life With attractive buffet decorations and presentations fitting the occasion, setting the mood with our mouthwatering delicacies. We treat every function as a treat for our customers.
